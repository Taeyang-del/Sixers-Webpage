Comedy Corner

A businessman finds a magic lamp and rubs it. Out pops a genie. The genie says, “I’ll grant you three wishes, but whatever you wish for, your worst enemy gets double.” The man thinks for a second and says: “Okay… first wish: I want a billion dollars.” The genie snaps his fingers — poof! A billion dollars in the man’s account… and two billion in his enemy’s. “Second wish: I want a mansion.” Poof! Huge mansion appears… and his enemy gets two. The man thinks hard about the last wish he looks happy “For my final wish… I want you to scare me half to death.”

By Hannah • Sep 2025
